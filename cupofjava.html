Cup of Java: The South Lakes Computer Science Club!

Welcome to the homepage for Cup of Java! This is where you can find a bunch of information that I (Jason Watkins) happened to put up!

General Information

Who are we?

We are computer science students who want to practice and expand their skills in java

What do we do?

Mainly, we practice for and compete in competitions. This year, we're also teaching some new skills and working on long-term projects that we can put up here!

Where/When does this all happen?

We have meetings on Wednesdays after-school in room 462, hosted by Mrs. Ladwig. Come by sometime!

Leadership:

  • President: Jason Watkins
  • Vice-President: Jack Thomas

FAQ

How do I join?

Just show up to one of our meetings! You can also put your info into the interest form.

What are these "competitions"?

We compete by creating programs that solve problems, usually reading data and creating an output. We're planning on these competing in competitions sponsored by the following:

  • Virginia Tech
  • University of Virginia
  • Lockheed Martin
  • University of Maryland
  • Thomas Jefferson High School
