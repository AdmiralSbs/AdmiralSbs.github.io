Cup of Java: The South Lakes Computer Science Club!

Welcome to the homepage for Cup of Java! This is where you will find important information on the club and how you can get involved.

General Information

Who are we?

We are computer science students who want to practice and expand our skills in java.

What do we do?

Mainly, we practice for and compete in competitions. This year, we're also teaching new skills and working on long-term projects that we can display here!

Where/When does this all happen?

We have meetings on Wednesdays after-school in Room 462, hosted by Mrs. Ladwig. Come by sometime!
